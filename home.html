Web Applications on SAP

Introducing SAP systems to the web with agility and beauty.

Blue Sierra Labs is an ABAP consultancy that builds custom applications for SAP systems. We are a small team of ABAP and web developer/designer who create SAP applications in ABAP while crafting reliable cross-device front-ends.

Look Good on Any Device.

Technology often advances (or wears out) faster than user needs change. Maybe a shop's monitors are replaced with rugged tablets or a sales force upgrades to the new iPhone. By using adaptive design techniques and the strongest UI frameworks, our interfaces will not break if you change devices. We guarantee it.

Connect to any SAP system.

If your organization does not have NetWeaver Gateway, or if you are not running an ECC 6.0 system, there are resources available to deliver high-quality SAP connectivity to your users. We will work with you to create the web or RFC connectivity you need.
